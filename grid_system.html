Ruby Hall Clinic, Pune, was founded by Dr. K. B. Grant and started as a small four-bedded nursing home. Over time, it developed into one of Pune’s major multispeciality hospitals. The institution expanded its services, infrastructure, and specialties, eventually becoming a large healthcare centre with branches and outreach facilities in different locations. Today, it is recognised for advanced treatment, medical education, and accredited quality healthcare services.
