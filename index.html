About our Hospital

Ruby Hall Clinic is one of Pune's leading multispecialty hospitals, established in 1959 by Dr. Keki B. Grant. From a small nursing home, it has grown into a trusted healthcare network. The hospital has major campuses at Sassoon Road, Hinjawadi, and Wanowrie. It is accredited by NABH and NABL, ensuring high standards of patient care. Ruby Hall Clinic is also a teaching hospital committed to medical education and excellence.
