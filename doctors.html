Dr. Arjun Mehta

Consultant – Cardiac Sciences & Coronary Care (Heart Care)

Dr. Arjun Mehta is an experienced cardiologist specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of heart diseases. He has expertise in preventive cardiology, coronary artery disease, heart failure, hypertension, and cardiac emergencies. Dedicated to delivering compassionate, patient-centered care, Dr. Mehta focuses on helping patients maintain optimal heart health through advanced diagnostic techniques and personalized treatment plans.

Dr. Vikram Rao

Consultant – Neuro Sciences (Neurology & Neurosurgery)

Dr. Vikram Rao is a skilled specialist in Neurology and Neurosurgery with expertise in treating disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system. His areas of interest include stroke management, epilepsy, movement disorders, brain tumors, and spinal conditions. He is committed to providing comprehensive neurological care using the latest medical and surgical advancements.

Dr. Karan Patel

Consultant – Orthopaedics & Joint Replacement

Dr. Karan Patel specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of bone, joint, and musculoskeletal disorders. With expertise in joint replacement, sports injuries, fractures, and arthritis management, he provides advanced orthopedic care focused on restoring mobility, reducing pain, and improving quality of life.

Dr. Sameer Joshi

Consultant – Orthopaedics & Joint Replacement

Dr. Sameer Joshi is an accomplished orthopedic surgeon with extensive experience in hip and knee replacement surgeries, trauma care, and minimally invasive orthopedic procedures. He is dedicated to helping patients regain movement and lead active, pain-free lives through personalized treatment plans.

Dr. Sneha Kulkarni

Consultant – Diabetology & Endocrinology

Dr. Sneha Kulkarni specializes in the management of diabetes, thyroid disorders, hormonal imbalances, and other endocrine conditions. She believes in preventive healthcare and works closely with patients to develop customized treatment strategies that promote long-term health and wellness

Dr. Priya Deshmukh

Consultant – Breast Surgery

Dr. Priya Deshmukh is a dedicated breast surgeon with expertise in the diagnosis and surgical management of both benign and malignant breast diseases. She is committed to providing compassionate care, early detection, and personalized treatment options using modern surgical techniques.

Dr. Riya Kapoor

Consultant – Gastroenterology & Digestive Diseases

Dr. Riya Kapoor specializes in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of digestive system disorders. Her expertise includes liver diseases, acid reflux, inflammatory bowel disease, gastrointestinal infections, and digestive health. She emphasizes accurate diagnosis and evidence-based treatment for improved patient outcomes.

Dr. Neha Sharma

Consultant – Cancer Services (Medical, Surgical & Radiation Oncology)

Dr. Neha Sharma is an experienced oncologist dedicated to providing comprehensive cancer care across medical, surgical, and radiation oncology. She focuses on personalized treatment plans, early cancer detection, and multidisciplinary care to ensure the best possible outcomes while supporting patients throughout their treatment journey.
