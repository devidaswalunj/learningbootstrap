Dr. Amol Jadhav

Dr. Amol – Consultant HPB, Liver Transplant & Advanced Laparoscopic Surgeon Dr. Amol is a highly skilled Consultant specializing in Hepato-Pancreato-Biliary (HPB) Surgery, Liver Transplantation, and Advanced Laparoscopic Surgery. With extensive clinical experience and advanced surgical expertise, he is dedicated to providing comprehensive, patient-centered care using the latest evidence-based techniques. He has expertise in the diagnosis and surgical management of complex diseases affecting the liver, pancreas, gallbladder, and bile ducts. Dr. Amol is proficient in performing advanced minimally invasive (laparoscopic) procedures, enabling faster recovery, reduced postoperative pain, shorter hospital stays, and improved patient outcomes.
