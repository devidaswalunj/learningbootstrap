Our Services

Department Description
Cardiac Sciences & Coronary Care (Heart Care) Comprehensive diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of heart diseases including coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and cardiac emergencies. Services include ECG, Echocardiography, Angiography, Angioplasty, heart surgeries, and 24/7 Coronary Care Unit (CCU).
Cancer Services (Medical, Surgical & Radiation Oncology) Complete cancer care through Medical Oncology, Surgical Oncology, and Radiation Oncology. Treatments include chemotherapy, immunotherapy, targeted therapy, advanced cancer surgeries, radiation therapy, cancer screening, rehabilitation, and palliative care.
Neuro Sciences (Neurology & Neurosurgery) Specialized care for brain, spine, and nervous system disorders including stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson’s disease, brain tumors, spinal disorders, and trauma. Services include neurological diagnosis, minimally invasive neurosurgery, neurocritical care, and rehabilitation.
Orthopaedics & Joint Replacement Advanced treatment for bone, joint, muscle, and ligament disorders. Services include fracture management, arthritis treatment, sports injury care, arthroscopy, knee and hip replacement, spinal care, physiotherapy, and rehabilitation.
Gastroenterology & Digestive Diseases Expert diagnosis and treatment of digestive system disorders including acid reflux, ulcers, IBS, liver diseases, pancreatitis, and gallbladder conditions. Services include endoscopy, colonoscopy, nutritional counseling, and advanced gastrointestinal care.

Patients' Frequently Asked Questions

Cardiac Sciences & Coronary Care provides comprehensive diagnosis, treatment, and management of heart-related conditions, including coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and emergency cardiac care.

Cancer Services include Medical Oncology (chemotherapy, immunotherapy, targeted therapy), Surgical Oncology (cancer surgeries), and Radiation Oncology (radiation therapy) for comprehensive cancer care.

Neuro Sciences includes Neurology for diagnosing and treating disorders of the brain, spine, and nerves, and Neurosurgery for surgical treatment of neurological conditions.

Orthopaedics & Joint Replacement focuses on treating bone, joint, muscle, and ligament conditions, including fractures, arthritis, sports injuries, and joint replacement surgeries.

Gastroenterology & Digestive Diseases specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the digestive system, including the stomach, intestines, liver, pancreas, and gallbladder.
